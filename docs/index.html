EIP-8038 Repricing Analysis

EIP-8038 reprices EVM state-access operations — cold and warm storage and account access, plus account and storage writes. This site re-derives those gas costs empirically: NNLS regressions on client benchmark runtimes, anchored at 100 Mgas/s, taking the worst-case client per parameter. See the methodology page for the full pipeline.

Of the 9 gas parameters analysed, 8 warrant an increase and 1 could be reduced relative to the current cost table. See the new-gas proposal for the per-parameter breakdown, or trends for how these estimates have moved across runs.

Preliminary results. These parameters are not final: the underlying measurements are still being refined as execution clients optimize their state-access paths. As those optimizations land, per-client runtimes should converge — which may shift the worst-case selection and the resulting gas parameters.
